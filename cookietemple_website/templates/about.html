History

Cookietemple was initially developed by Lukas Heumos (GitHub/Twitter) and Philipp Ehmele (GitHub/Twitter) since the beginning of 2020.

We noticed the lack of standardization in projects structure even in the same domain/application space. This makes it harder, especially for new developers, to join existing projects and work together in a collaborative environment.

It was our aim to contribute cookietemple as a tool to tackle this problem to the open-source community. The inital release has been in August 2020 and we're planning to consistently develope cookietemple further together with the open-source community.
